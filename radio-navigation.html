Q2.

How many VOR stations are required for RNAV operations?

  • a) 1
  • b) 2
  • c) 3
  • d) 4

‚úÖ Answer: b ‚Äî 2

  • VOR = VHF Omnidirectional Range
  • Operates on 108-118 MHz
  • Provides 360 radials (like spokes of a wheel)
  • 1 VOR = position line
  • 2 VORs = position fix (intersection)

For RNAV: minimum 2 VORs for area navigation.

Q6.

Primary radar (PSR) detects:

  • a) Transponder signals only
  • b) Reflected ground returns (targets)
  • d) Only cooperative targets

‚úÖ Answer: b

  • PSR = Primary Surveillance Radar
  • Detects reflected energy from aircraft
  • No cooperation needed from aircraft
  • Also called "skin paint" or "search radar"
  • SSR = Secondary Surveillance Radar
  • Requires transponder (Mode A/C/S)

ASR = Airport Surveillance Radar (PSR + SSR combined)
