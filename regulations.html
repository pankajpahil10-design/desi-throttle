Q1. DGCA stands for:

a) Director General of Civil Aviation
b) Director General of Certified Aviation
c) Department of Government Civil Aviation
d) Directorate General of Civil Aviation
Answer: a) Director General of Civil Aviation

DGCA is the regulatory body for civil aviation in India.

Q2. CAR stands for:

a) Civil Aviation Requirements
b) Civil Aircraft Regulations
c) Certified Aviation Rules
d) Commercial Aviation Regulations
Answer: a) Civil Aviation Requirements

CARs are regulatory documents issued by DGCA for various aspects of civil aviation.
