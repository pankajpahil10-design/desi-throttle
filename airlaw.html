Questions from Oxford Aviation Books | Home

1. Low intensity obstacle light colour:

a. Blue
b. Steady red
c. Yellow
d. Flashing red
Answer: b. Steady red

Low intensity obstacle lights are steady red.

2. Low intensity obstruction lights on fixed objects:

a. Flashing green
b. Flashing yellow
c. Steady red
d. Steady blue
Answer: c. Steady red

3. OIS stands for:

a. Obstacle Interference Slope
b. Obstacle Identification Surface
c. Obstacle Inner Surface
d. Obstacle Identification Slope
Answer: b. Obstacle Identification Surface

4. Colour of vehicles moving routinely on aerodrome:

a. Flashing yellow
b. Flashing red
c. Flashing blue
d. Steady red
Answer: a. Flashing yellow

5. Emergency vehicles on maneuvering area colour:

a. Green
b. Dayglo orange
c. Single conspicuous colour - red or yellowish green
d. White and red chequered
Answer: c. Single conspicuous colour - red or yellowish green

6. An en-route obstacle is located:

a. Within 15 km radius of aerodrome
b. Outside 5 nm from controlled airspace
c. Beyond 15 km radius of aerodrome
d. In cruise phase
Answer: c. Beyond 15 km radius of aerodrome

7. Documents for aircraft entry to a country:

a. Must be typewritten
b. Can be hand-written in block capitals
c. Accepted at discretion of state
d. Electronic data format only
Answer: b. Can be hand-written in block capitals in ink or indelible pencil

8. Oral declaration for baggage:

a. Never acceptable
b. Yes
c. Only crew baggage
d. Only passenger baggage
Answer: b. Yes (acceptable)

9. General Declaration signed by:

a. PIC only
b. PIC or Agent
c. Any flight crew
d. Operator
Answer: b. PIC or Agent

10. Advance notification for foreign aircraft over territory:

a. 2 hours before arrival
b. 4 hours before arrival
c. 1 hour before arrival
d. 12 hours before ETA
Answer: a. At least 2 hours in advance

11. ICAO Annex for entry/departure of persons:

a. Annex 6
b. Annex 15
c. Annex 9
d. Annex 8
Answer: c. Annex 9 (Facilitation)

12. Distress light observed at night:

a. Flash landing/nav lights twice
b. Fly low and flash lights
c. Fly circles above
d. Fly triangular pattern
Answer: a. Flash landing lights or navigation lights twice

13. SAR signal 'X' on ground means:

a. We need help
b. We are OK
c. We need medical assistance
d. We have gone away
Answer: c. We need medical assistance

14. SAR services provided:

a. During FIR hours
b. 24 hour basis
c. 2 hrs before first to 2 hrs after last flight
d. During commercial + military ops
Answer: b. On a 24 hour basis

15. DGCA stands for:

a. Director General of Civil Aviation
b. Directorate General of Civil Aviation
c. Department of Civil Aviation
d. Director General Certified Aviation
Answer: a. Director General of Civil Aviation

16. CAR stands for:

a. Civil Aviation Requirements
b. Civil Aircraft Regulations
c. Certified Aviation Rules
d. Commercial Aviation Regulations
Answer: a. Civil Aviation Requirements

17. Minimum VFR visibility:

a. 1 km
b. 3 km
c. 5 km
d. 10 km
Answer: c. 5 km (3 nm)

18. ICAO standard pressure:

a. 1000 hPa
b. 1013.25 hPa
c. 1015 hPa
d. 1020 hPa
Answer: b. 1013.25 hPa (29.92 inHg)

19. VFR cloud base minimum:

a. 500 ft
b. 1000 ft
c. 1500 ft
d. 2000 ft
Answer: b. 1000 ft AGL

20. Flight visibility below 10,000ft in Class G:

a. 1 km
b. 3 km
c. 5 km
d. 10 km
Answer: c. 5 km (below 10,000ft AMSL in Class G)
