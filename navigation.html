Q1. The earth is a sphere - true or false?

a) True
b) False - it's an oblate spheroid
c) False - it's flat
d) False - it's oval
Answer: b) False - it's an oblate spheroid

Earth is an oblate spheroid - slightly flattened at the poles due to rotation.

Q2. Great Circle represents:

a) Shortest distance between two points
b) Longest distance
c) Same latitude
d) Magnetic heading
Answer: a) Shortest distance between two points

Great Circle is the shortest path between two points on a sphere - used for long distance routes.

Q3. 1 NM (Nautical Mile) equals:

a) 6080 feet
b) 5280 feet
c) 7000 feet
d) 6000 feet
Answer: a) 6080 feet

1 Nautical Mile = 1852 meters = 6080 feet (approximately 1 minute of latitude).
